About Me

I’m a data analyst and AI enthusiast originally from Tajikistan — a small, mountainous country in Central Asia. Growing up in a place where access to advanced technology was limited only fueled my curiosity. I’ve always been fascinated by how things work — from the cosmos to computers — and that early passion led me to pursue a future in data, artificial intelligence, and systems design.

Achieving my dream of studying in the United States, I earned my degree from the University of Nebraska at Kearney, where I focused on data analytics and machine learning. During that time, I built real-world tools — including a predictive model for healthcare billing and an AI-powered chatbot that integrates document ingestion, OpenAI GPT, and vector search for dynamic, role-based support.

I currently serve as a Graduate Assistant for the Office of Graduate Studies and Academic Innovation at UNK. In this role, I support academic program development, collect and analyze student success data, assist in graduate marketing efforts, and help enhance institutional effectiveness through data-driven reporting.

I’m passionate about using technology to make systems smarter and more human-centered. Whether it’s solving technical problems or making complex processes more accessible, I aim to be part of the next generation shaping how data and AI are used to improve everyday life.

Projects

UNK AI Chatbot (Capstone Project)

Tech Stack: Python, Flask, WebSockets, React, Tailwind CSS, MongoDB, Pinecone, OpenAI API

A full-stack conversational AI assistant designed to provide instant, accurate answers from the University of Nebraska at Kearney (UNK) website.

  • Document Ingestion: Crawls and parses data from the UNK site into a searchable format.
  • Vector Search: Uses Pinecone to enable semantic, context-aware query matching.
  • Real-Time Chat: Supports live group messaging with WebSocket integration.
  • Role-Based Access: Different UIs and permissions for staff vs. students.
  • Modern UI: Responsive frontend with markdown rendering and clean design.
  • Smart AI Layer: OpenAI GPT used to generate relevant, conversational responses.
